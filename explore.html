Experience Staines’s Charm

Set on the banks of the River Thames, Staines blends boutique riverside charm with modern convenience, just 3.7 miles from Heathrow. Explore the historic town centre, vibrant Two Rivers shopping, and nearby attractions like Thorpe Park and Windsor Castle, perfect for a relaxing getaway or family adventure.

Local Attractions

Discover Staines

River Thames

Enjoy scenic riverside walks and boat trips along the picturesque River Thames, just steps from the hotel.

Windsor Castle

Visit the historic Windsor Castle, a royal residence with stunning architecture, a short drive from Staines.

Thorpe Park

Experience thrilling rides and family fun at Thorpe Park, a popular theme park near Staines.
