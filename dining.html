Nestled by the banks of the River Thames, the Riverside Restaurant & Bar at Staines Hotel by Sunday offers unforgettable dining experiences. Enjoy modern British cuisine with seasonal menus showcasing fresh, local ingredients.

Our Restaurant

The Riverside Restaurant & Bar offers stunning views of the River Thames with modern British flavors using fresh, local ingredients. Enjoy seasonal menus in the restaurant, cocktails and light bites in the bar, or a signature buffet breakfast, accommodating up to 100 guests in an elegant setting.

The Lounge area is also available for dining.
