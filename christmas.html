Festive Celebrations

A Magical Christmas in Staines

Celebrate the festive season at Staines Hotel by Sunday, nestled on the banks of the River Thames. Our elegant Conservatory and Riverside Restaurant offer the perfect setting for party nights, private gatherings, and Christmas Day lunch. Enjoy seasonal menus, festive décor, and a warm atmosphere, with 88 rooms for a memorable holiday escape.
