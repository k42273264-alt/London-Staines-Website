Our Story

Staines Hotel by Sunday

Charming Riverside

Today, Staines Hotel blends its historic riverside allure with contemporary elegance, offering 88 spacious rooms, riverside dining, and versatile event spaces just 3.7 miles from Heathrow Airport.

19th Century: Riverside Heritage

Originally established in the 19th century, Staines Hotel by Sunday has long been a cherished riverside landmark, reflecting the historical charm of Staines-upon-Thames with its picturesque setting.
